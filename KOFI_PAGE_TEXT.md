## Short bio

TELAUDIT is a free local Windows tool for automatic IPBX backup analysis, auditing and documentation.

It generates PDF, HTML, CSV and diagram exports without telemetry, tracking or data collection.

## About section

TELAUDIT is designed to help administrators and telecom integrators document IPBX systems faster.

The software runs locally on Windows. Your IPBX backups are not sent to the Internet.

TELAUDIT is distributed free of charge as donationware. If it saves you time or helps you document a system, you can support the project here.

Donations are optional and help with development, maintenance and future improvements. They do not include guaranteed support or guaranteed updates.
