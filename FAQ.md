# FAQ

## Is TELAUDIT free?

Yes. TELAUDIT is distributed free of charge as donationware. Donations are optional.

## Does TELAUDIT send data to the Internet?

No. TELAUDIT performs the analysis locally and does not intentionally send IPBX backup data, reports or audit results to the Internet.

## Does TELAUDIT include telemetry or tracking?

No. There is no telemetry, tracking, advertising or analytics.

## Which systems are supported?

The current beta focuses mainly on Xorcom / Ombutel-style backups. More parsers may be added progressively.

## Can I use TELAUDIT on production backups?

Yes, but generated information must always be reviewed and verified. TELAUDIT is an assistance and documentation tool, not a replacement for professional review.

## Is support included?

No guaranteed support is included. Feedback and bug reports are welcome, but response times and updates are not guaranteed.

## Can I redistribute TELAUDIT?

You may share the official download link. Do not rebrand, resell or present TELAUDIT as your own software.

## Is the source code public?

The public GitHub repository is intended as a project page and release channel. The source code may not be included in public releases unless explicitly stated.
